(Un)Locker, Again

Your locker in the police station's locker room contains several of the items and equipment you'll need to do your job. The locker combination (originally hidden in the Police Quest 3 manual) is 7-7-6.

"Penal" Code...*snicker*

Since this IS a police simulation, you'll have to book offenders on various charges when it comes time to throw 'em in the slammer, or write them a ticket (using your police cruiser's onboard computer). Either way, you'll be prompted to enter the appropriate violation code to properly book/ticket the offender. Using the following list of violations, look up the charge that applies to the perp in question, and enter it into the provided keypad.
