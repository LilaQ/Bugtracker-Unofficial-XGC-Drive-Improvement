Flower Power

During the sequence of events at Ot Moor, you will be tested on your knowledge of "the language of the flowers" by having to answer several riddles. Look here to learn what each flower represents, then use the info when answering the riddles.

Additionally, the Message of the Rose can come in very handy for crossing the Moor (lake) itself, assuming you have the right item...

Holy Crap

At two later points in the game, you'll once again be faced with riddles that demand knowledge from the game manual, this time of the various Goddesses. Here is (almost) all of the information you need.

Notice I said "ALMOST all". In addition to the manual, you'll need to learn the symbols of the goddesses. This information can be discovered in the game itself; be sure to ask about it to the right people.

I Don't Like Spam

Although this isn't copy protection at all, it's such a great easter egg that I'll mention it here anyway. At the beginning of the game, go into the castle treasury, then type

HAM AND JAM AND SPAM A LOT
