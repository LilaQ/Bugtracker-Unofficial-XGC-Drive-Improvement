Other Sierra games

ATTENTION: There are a couple of Sierra games for which I could not come up with any manuals or CP info. These games have been marked as such. If anyone out there has any manual scans for these games, PLEASE contact me so I can add the info.

Select the game you need help with below:

Freddy Pharkas, Frontier Pharmacist
Al Lowe and Josh Mandel's wild west comedy masterpiece!

EcoQuest: The Search for Cetus
Sierra's venture into edutainment. Join young econaut Adam as he saves the creatures of the sea, and learns about the environment in the process.

EcoQuest 2: Lost Secret of the Rainforest
Adam must once again lend a hand to the environment, by protecting a tropical rainforest from a gold-hunting explorer.

Conquests of Camelot
Relive one of the most famous quests of all time, as King Arthur sets forth to find the Holy Grail!

Conquest of the Longbow: The Legend of Robin Hood
Live the life of history's most famous bandit as he fights to free the land from the tyrrany of Prince John!

Codename: ICEMAN
Navy officer John Westland must carry out a covert submarine mission to rescue an American diplomat, before the Cold War starts heating up...

Laura Bow: The Colonel's Bequest
A murder mystery, taking place at a spooky plantation in the 1920's. College student Laura Bow is invited by her friend Lillian to come along to what has to be the most dysfunctional family gathering ever. The only problem is, various members of the family begin to mysteriously disappear!

Laura Bow 2: The Dagger of Amon Ra
Now graduated and pursuing a career as a reporter, Laura gets her first big break... and lands right in the middle of another murder mystery!
