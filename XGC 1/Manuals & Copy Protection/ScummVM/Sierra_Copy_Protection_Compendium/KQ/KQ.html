King's Quest series

Select the King's Quest game you need help with below:

King's Quest I: Quest for the Crown
King Edward is dying! Can his loyal knight Graham recover Daventry's three sacred treasures, and earn the crown for himself?

King's Quest II: Romancing the Throne
It's good to be the king! But Graham is lonely, and sets out on a quest to find the woman of his dreams.

King's Quest III: To Heir is Human
Gwydion, a slave to the wizard Mannannan, ventures out into the unknown world to defeat his evil master...and learn of his shocking destiny.

King's Quest IV: The Perils of Rosella
King Graham has fallen deathly ill...now it's Rosella's turn to follow in her father's footsteps, to find the cure that will save his life.

King's Quest V: Absence Makes the Heart Go Yonder
Just when Graham thinks his questing days are over, his home and family are abducted! Graham dons his adventurer's cap once more as he sets out to track down the culprit and rescue his loved ones!

King's Quest VI: Heir Today, Gone Tomorrow
Alexander sails to lands unknown to woo the woman he loves. But, all is not as it seems in the Land of the Green Isles...

King's Quest VII: The Princeless Bride
Queen Valanice searches desperately for her missing daughter, even as Rosella attempts to stop an evil fairie bent on destruction!

King's Quest: The Mask of Eternity
The entire population of Daventry has been turned to stone! Connor, a lowly peasant, must gather the shards of the Mask of Eternity to restore order to the world, and save his homeland.
