Chemistry 101

Throughout the game, Dr. Cranium will be your primary supplier of healing and antivenom potions...you can recieve one of each for free, once a day. There's a catch, however...before he'll give you any at all, you must help him complete the formula. When the Doc gives you a compound name, look it up in the chart below and enter the proper elemental formula.
